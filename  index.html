About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival game where you must outlast waves of fearsome monsters. With each passing minute, enemies grow stronger and faster. Collect upgrades, unlock new abilities, and become the ultimate survivor. This game runs directly in your browser—no downloads needed!

Game Features

  • Fast-paced monster survival gameplay
  • Power-ups and skill upgrades
  • Endless wave progression
  • Compatible on PC, Mac, tablets, and smartphones
  • No download required – play instantly!

How to Play

Use your keyboard and mouse or touchscreen to navigate and attack enemies. Survive as long as possible, collect XP, and level up your character to unleash devastating abilities. Quick reflexes and smart upgrades are key to victory.
